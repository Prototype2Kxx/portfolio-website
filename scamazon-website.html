Scamazon E-Commerce Website

A fully responsive e-commerce prototype featuring a full shopping experience and interactive UI.

Engaging Homepage

The homepage features a hero video banner, category previews, and a sleek product carousel to showcase top items.

Interactive Product Modals

Clicking on a product opens a detailed modal with images, specifications, and an option to add to cart.

Functional Shopping Cart

The site includes a live updating shopping cart UI, powered by JavaScript and stored with localStorage to persist across pages.

Simple Checkout Flow

Scamazon features a clean and intuitive checkout page for submitting customer information and completing a mock order.

Smart Search Bar

The search bar offers live suggestions and matches across categories, helping users quickly find relevant products.
