About Me

Hello, I’m George Condrea, an aspiring software and cybersecurity professional based in Swanage, Dorset. I’ll be starting a degree in Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics in September 2025, and I’ve recently completed an Access to Higher Education Diploma in Computing. This portfolio showcases the work I’ve done so far, including personal projects, skills I’ve developed, and my career progression into software and security.

I started my career in professional kitchen environments, where I spent close to ten years honing my creativity, discipline, and pressure-testing skills. I enjoyed working in the kitchen and was successful on my own level, but I couldn’t help feeling drawn to new horizons, something that involved problem-solving and working with technology. That's how I shifted into computing and cybersecurity. It's been tough but well worth it, and I am excited about where it's going.

Although my work is not completely AI-free, I use tools like ChatGPT and Claude Code to help refine, polish, and improve my work. These tools support my learning rather than replacing the thinking and problem-solving behind each project.
