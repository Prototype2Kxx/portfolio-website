02 / background

My Story.

The journey from professional kitchens to cybersecurity labs was unconventional, but every step was deliberate.

View Projects → Full CV

Hello, I'm George Condrea, an aspiring software and cybersecurity professional based in Swanage, Dorset. I'm studying a degree in Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics at Bournemouth University, having recently completed an Access to Higher Education Diploma in Computing. This portfolio showcases the work I've done so far: personal projects, skills I've developed, and my career progression into software and security.

I started my career in professional kitchen environments, where I spent close to ten years honing my creativity, discipline, and composure under pressure. I enjoyed working in the kitchen and was successful on my own level, but I couldn't help feeling drawn to new horizons, something that involved problem-solving and working with technology. That's how I shifted into computing and cybersecurity. It's been tough but well worth it, and I am excited about where it's going.

Although my work is not completely AI-free, I use tools like ChatGPT and Claude Code to help refine, polish, and improve my work. These tools support my learning rather than replacing the thinking and problem-solving behind each project.
